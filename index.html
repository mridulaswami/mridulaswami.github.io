ABOUT

The Startup STARLINK consist of unique technology that will help to fill the connectivity gap of internet speeds between urban and rural areas. And this tech holds the satellites that will sit closer to earth to reduce latency and lasers to enhance and boost the internet speeds. Starlink is a satellite internet constellation , that will consist of thousands of mass produced small satellites in Low Earth Orbit. The satellite were expected to be in the smallest- class of 100 to 500 kg mas and are intended to be in Low Earth Orbit. The satellites will employ optical inter satellite links and phrased array beam forming and digital processing technologies. The satellites will be mass produced at a much lower cost per unit of capability. Our Starlink satellites use Hall effect thrusters with krypton gas as the reaction mass for orbit raising and station keeping. They tend to exhibit significantly higher erosion of the flow channel compared to a similar electric propulsion system operated with xenon but at a lower propellent cost. The system will be linked to flat user terminals which will have phrased array antennas and track the satellites.the terminals can be mounted anywhere.

SERVICES AND VISION

• We ensure to provide satellite internet connectivity to undeserved areas of the planet, as well as provide competitively priced service to urban areas.
• Our starlink satellites are over 60 times closer to earth than traditional satellites ,resulting in lower latency and the ability to support service typically not possible with traditional satellite internet.
• The downloading speeds will be 300mbps or double the top speeds users can currently get on the beta service.
